Terms and Conditions

Please read our terms of service carefully before placing an order.

1. General Agreement

By placing an order with Agender Printing Press, you agree to the terms listed below. These terms apply to all printing services, including digital, offset, large format, and custom merchandise.

2. Orders and Deposits

A non-refundable deposit of 70% is required to commence any printing job. The remaining balance is due upon collection or delivery of the finished goods. For bulk orders, full payment may be required upfront depending on the nature of the project.

3. Artwork and Files

Clients are responsible for ensuring that all artwork submitted is print-ready (300 DPI, CMYK color mode, and correct dimensions). We are not responsible for spelling errors, low-resolution images, or color variations caused by incorrect file formatting.

4. Turnaround Time

Estimated turnaround times are 3-5 business days for standard orders. Rush services are available for an additional fee. Delays caused by artwork issues or approval wait times are not included in the turnaround calculation.

5. Returns and Refunds

Due to the custom nature of our products, we do not offer returns or refunds once the printing process has begun. If there is a manufacturing defect, please contact us within 24 hours of receiving your order for a resolution.

6. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Any personal information or digital files shared with us are used solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order and will not be shared with third parties.
