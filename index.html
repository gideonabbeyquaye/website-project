Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our printing services.

What file formats do you accept?

+

We accept PDF, AI, PSD, EPS, TIFF, and high-resolution JPG/PNG files. For best results, please ensure all fonts are outlined and images are at least 300 DPI.

What is your standard turnaround time?

+

Standard turnaround time is 3-5 business days for most digital and offset orders. Large format and specialty items may take 5-7 business days. Rush services are available upon request.

Do you offer design services?

+

Yes! We have a team of professional graphic designers who can help bring your vision to life, from logo creation to full marketing material layouts.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

+

For digital printing (business cards, flyers), we have low minimums. For offset printing and custom merchandise, minimums may vary to ensure cost-effectiveness. Contact us for specific details.
